SCENE 02 INT. MUMBAI LOCAL - DAY
Yeh kahani hai ek self-taught designer ki…

Doors never quite close on the 8:42 fast local - and neither does my curiosity. Six-plus years ago I boarded design without a formal ticket: self-taught, stubborn, and certain that good interfaces should feel like a kind stranger giving you directions. Today I'm a Senior Associate UI/UX Designer at NPCI, drawing the rails beneath India's digital money - from Retail & Programmable CBDC to certification systems that let the world plug into UPI, iUPI, IMPS, AePS, BBPS . I turn knotty fintech into something a person can simply use, balancing business goals, regulation, and the quiet dignity of a clean screen.
